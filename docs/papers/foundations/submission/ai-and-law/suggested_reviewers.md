## 1. Kevin D. Ashley
- **Affiliation:** University of Pittsburgh, Pittsburgh, PA, USA
- **Degree:** PhD in Computer Science, University of Massachusetts (1988)
- **Email:** ashley@pitt.edu
- **Expertise:** AI and legal analytics, case-based reasoning, legal argumentation, computational models of legal reasoning
- **Justification:** Professor Ashley is the leading authority on computational case-based legal reasoning, and his monograph *Artificial Intelligence and Legal Analytics* (Cambridge, 2017) — cited in the manuscript — established the field's standard reference. He is uniquely positioned to evaluate whether the judicial complex and the pathfinding formalization of litigation represent a genuine advance over existing computational law frameworks. His dual appointment in Law and Intelligent Systems enables him to assess both the mathematical formalism and its legal fidelity.

- **Email:** h.prakken@uu.nl
- **Expertise:** Legal argumentation, formal models of legal reasoning, deontic logic, AI and law

- **Degree:** PhD, European University Institute
- **Email:** giovanni.sartor@unibo.it
- **Expertise:** Computational legal theory, Hohfeldian analysis, deontic logic, legal ontologies, normative systems
